Maldives

I had visited in june 25th in 2019

Maldives is an island. Acoording to me it is the best place you can visit in the summer. The capital of Maldives is Male. There are many other islands as well, but there active valcanoes in the most of them. Hence, Ther are many resorts in Male comparing to the other islands. I stayed in one of them. It was a world-class experience. If you are planning to visit there, you may wanna exchange ruppes into dollars as dollars are the currency used in Maldives. There were many water games. I tried gocarting and jetski.Each sports game was 8 dollars. It was very fun. The food was mostly western but there were also a few indian dishes. there were two types of houses where you can stay in, Water villa & Land Villa. Water villas are those where the villa is on the water whereas, land villa is where the villa is on the land!! I lived on the land villa where I got the beach view. There were not only water games but also outdoor games like Squash, Billiards, Table Tennis and so on. And of caurse ther was a swimming pool which was big enough for everyong on the island to fit in!! Hence, Maldives is a place that you must visit at least once in your lifetime.
